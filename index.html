About the Show

What is Exponential Promise?

Exponential Promise explores the difference between a thriving future—where industrial-scale AI helps "solve everything"—and a dystopian one where we solve math and physics but fail to address the crises that abundance depends on. Each short series dives deep into a single humanity-scale issue, bringing together founders, clinicians, policymakers, and systems thinkers to ask what it would actually take to move exponentially faster on that problem.

  • Short, 4–6-episode series per topic.
  • Focus on under-weighted, civilization-scale problems.
  • Real stories plus expert insight and practical paths to action.
  • Framed by Exponential Ventures' thesis and experience backing founders in these spaces.

"We know we can't do everything we would like to. We focus on a few problem spaces where we can help catalyze investable solutions."

Debut Series

AMR & Sepsis

Our debut series looks at antimicrobial resistance and sepsis as quiet civilization-scale threats—and a test of whether we can keep the promise of exponential health.

"AMR & Sepsis" treats antimicrobial resistance and sepsis not as background clinical problems, but as threats that could roll back much of antibiotics' contribution to life expectancy and turn AI's exponential promise into a darker future. We'll talk with epidemiologists, economists, regulators, founders, and hospital leaders about why the bottlenecks are incentives and systems—not science—and how exponential organizations and better-aligned capital can accelerate solutions.

Planned episode arc

  • EP 01 The crisis and the Exponential Ventures framework
  • EP 02 AMR: the economics of urgency and broken incentives
  • EP 03 Sepsis: from bedside reality to hospital-scale adoption
  • EP 04 Regulatory speed: how modernization can unlock exponential health
  • EP 05 Vision 2030: what changes if we move together now

Coming Next

The Human Side of Abundance

Beyond biology and regulation, Exponential Promise will explore the emotional and social fractures that could make an AI-powered future more painful than prosperous if we leave them unaddressed.

Tolerance & Empathy

How do intolerance and the erosion of empathy destabilize societies—and what does it look like to rebuild them through storytelling, education, and new models of connection?

Grief

Unresolved grief quietly shapes decisions, relationships, and communities. What would change if we treated grief as a civilization-scale issue and built systems to help people process loss?

Anger

Anger is exploding in our homes, workplaces, and politics. We'll explore the 'anger epidemic' as a systems and brain-level problem—and the exponential tools that might help us regulate it at scale.

These are just some of the humanity-scale issue areas where Exponential Ventures is exploring and supporting investment opportunities. We can't do everything—so we focus where we can matter most.
